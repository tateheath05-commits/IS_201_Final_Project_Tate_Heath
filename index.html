Career Skills

Tate Heath has had a variety of jobs in his life.

Aerating

Tate started his own aerating business when he was a sophomore in high school. It was called 'Boss Co. Aerating' and had its own branding. Tate, his father, and his brothers grew the business to have over 200 clients by the time Tate left on his mission. They did this through door to door sales, mass emails, word of mouth advertising, and custom made yard signs. Boss Co. Aerating continues to be a Heath family business that has taught Tate and his brothers valuable life lessons such as accountability, hard-work, and problem-solving.

Real Estate

After returning from his mission, Tate grew interested in real estate, taking after his father. He has been attending the BYU Real Estate Association club every week throughout his freshman semester at BYU. He has participated in four different real estate case competitions. In January, he started a real estate internship for Prospera Growth Fund. All of these experiences have deepened his passion for real estate.

MTC Teacher

Tate developed a love for God, missionary work, and language learning while on his mission in Poland. Upon return, he immediately started working for the Missionary Training Center (MTC). He has taught Ukrainian and Polish to young missionaries while at the MTC. His roles have increased to include Polish mission leader tutoring and Ukrainian language evaluating. Through it all, Tate has continued to deepen his love for the gospel of Jesus Christ!

Tate's Timeline

See below for a brief synopsis of Tate's life.

  • 2014-2017

    A Happy Childhood

    Tate grew up as the oldest of four children - three boys and one girl. He loved playing sports, especially basketball and soccer. When Tate and his brother, Bryson were younger, they won a state free throw competition together in their respective age groups.

  • 2022-2023

    Tate for SBO President

    In Tate's junior year of high school, he ran for Student Body President under a campaign that featured Heath candy bars. He was voted in as the SBO President during his senior year of high school. Through his leadership and service, Tate went to countless sporting events, planned dances, put on fundraisers, and even spoke at graduation!

  • 2023-2025

    Called to Serve in Poland

    In July 2023, Tate left to serve a mission in Poland for the Lord, representing The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. He was called to speak Ukrainian and learned Polish and Russian while he was there. On his mission, Tate developed a love for God and His Son Jesus Christ, a stronger testimony of the Restoration of the gospel, and an admiration for the Ukrainian and Polish people.

  • December 2025

    Married to Love of His Life

    After a year of long distance and two years writing on missions, Tate and Brydee were sealed for time and all eternity in the St. George, UT temple. Brydee is the perfect match for Tate, complementing him in all the ways that can make him a better person. She is the love of his life and Tate is thrilled to be able to spend forever with the most perfect person!

Backpacking

Exploring trails, camping out, and enjoying the outdoors.

Tate has always loved being in the great outdoors. Backpacking, hiking, and camping are some of his favorite ways to spend time in nature and stay physically active. One of his favorite backpacking experiences was a backpacking trip from East Canyon resort (where he had a family trip) back to his house. There wasn't an official trail so they had to truly navigate the wilderness to find their way home.

Basketball

Staying active with pickup games and team competition.

Since Tate was little he has loved playing basketball. His dad used to take him and his younger brother to the local church gym to shoot free throws and practice drills in the morning before school started. Tate and his younger brother played a lot of one-on-one in their backyard basketball court which helped both of them get really good. Tate continues to love playing pick up ball with friends and shooting around with his wife, who is also a basketball player.

Pickleball

Quick matches with friends and a fun competitive pace.

Tate has grown a love for pickleball as he's spent countless hours playing with siblings and cousins at their semiannual family trip, East Canyon Resort. Tate enjoys pickleball for its fast gameplay, strategy, and social competition. Now, he likes to play whenever possible with his wife, siblings, cousins, and friends.

Soccer

The love of the game has been part of Tate’s life for years.

Since he was four years old, Tate has loved playing soccer! He can still remember when he scored four goals in one game as an 8 year old. Throughout his childhood, Tate played on various recreational and competitive soccer teams, including Bountiful High's soccer team. His main position on the pitch was defending midfielder. To this day, soccer is one of Tate's favorite team sports.

Spider-Man

A favorite hero and pop-culture interest for Tate.

Superheroes have been a long time interest of Tate's, and Spider-Man has been Tate's favorite hero for most of his life. He loves the character's humor, courage, and relatability. When he was little, he used to watch the Tobey Maguire Spider-Man trilogy all the time! One year for Christmas, Tate asked Santa for Spider-Man webslingers, thinking he could finally shoot real webs when he got them. To his surprise, the webslingers weren't the real thing and only shot rope.

Spikeball

Fast-paced games perfect for summer and group play.

Ever since his younger brother got a spikeball set for his birthday, Tate has loved the game of spikeball. Whether it's playing competitively with friends at his house or just playing for fun on the beach with his siblings and wife, Tate has a passion for spikeball. One of his favorite memories related to spikeball is when he beat his friend after having made the deal that if he beat him, his friend would have to get his first kiss.
